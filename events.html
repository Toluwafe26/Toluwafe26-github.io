Upcoming Event: A Night for Change

Join Us in Making a Difference

Date: 21/12/2023 | Time: 12pm | Location: Trinity Center, Bristol.

About the Event:

Save the Children invites you to a day filled with hope, compassion, and the shared goal of making a lasting impact on children's lives. "A Day for Change" is an event dedicated to raising awareness and funds for our action programs that strive to provide a better future for children around the world.


What to Expect:

Inspiring Speakers: Hear from passionate individuals who have witnessed firsthand the transformative power of your support.

Interactive Workshops: Engage in thought-provoking discussions and learn more about the challenges children face globally.

Live Entertainment: Enjoy an evening of entertainment, featuring performances that celebrate the resilience and spirit of children.

Silent Auction: Bid on exclusive items, with all proceeds directly supporting Save the Children's initiatives.

  • Spread the Word: Help us reach a wider audience by sharing the event details on social media. Use the hashtag #ANightforChange and let your network know about this opportunity to make a difference.

Your support can break the cycle of poverty and create a ripple effect that lasts for generations.

Join Us in Creating Lasting Change: Together, we can make a difference in the lives of children who need it most. Join us at "A Night for Change" and be a part of the movement to ensure every child has the opportunity to thrive.
