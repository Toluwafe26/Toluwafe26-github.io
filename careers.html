Save the children action program was created to help children all over the world have a better life and future.

Position: Community Development Coordinator

Organization: Save the Children Action Program

Location: United Kingdom

Save the Children Action Program is currently seeking a passionate and experienced individual to join our team as a Community Development Coordinator. As the Community Development Coordinator, you will play a crucial role in designing, implementing, and managing community-based programs aimed at improving the lives of children and families in need.

Key Responsibilities:

Program Design and Planning:

Collaborate with stakeholders to identify community needs and design development programs that address the unique challenges faced by children and families. Develop comprehensive project plans, ensuring alignment with Save the Children's mission and objectives.

Community Engagement:


Build and maintain strong relationships with community leaders, local organizations, and government agencies. Facilitate community meetings and workshops to gather input, promote awareness, and encourage participation in program activities.

Capacity Building:


Provide training and support to community members, empowering them to take an active role in program implementation and sustainability. Identify and leverage local resources to enhance community capacity for self-reliance.

Monitoring and Evaluation:


Establish robust monitoring and evaluation mechanisms to track the impact and effectiveness of community development programs. Regularly assess program outcomes and make data-driven adjustments for continuous improvement.

Advocacy and Networking:


Represent Save the Children Action Program in relevant forums and networks to advocate for the rights and well-being of children and families.

Collaborate with other NGOs, government agencies, and partners to amplify the impact of our collective efforts.

Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in social work, community development, international development, or a related field. Master's degree is a plus.
Proven experience in designing and implementing community development programs.

Strong understanding of child rights, protection, and community-based approaches.

Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

Ability to work collaboratively with diverse stakeholders.

How to Apply:


Interested candidates should submit their resume, cover letter, and three professional references to [savethechildrenprogram@gmail.com] by 5th of January 2024. Please include "Community Development Coordinator Application" in the subject line.

Save the Children Action Program is an equal opportunity employer and encourages candidates from all backgrounds to apply. We value diversity and inclusion in our workforce.
